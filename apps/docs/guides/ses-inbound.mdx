# SES Inbound Email

AgentDomain stores only structured text email. Raw MIME is transient.

## Flow

1. SES receives mail for `agent@domain`.
2. SES receipt rule writes raw MIME to S3.
3. S3/Lambda or an AWS-side forwarder posts the SES event to `/api/webhooks/ses/inbound`.
4. AgentDomain fetches the S3 object, extracts plain text and verification codes, inserts the structured row, then deletes the raw object.

Failed SES spam or virus verdicts are ignored. HTML, attachments, and raw MIME are not persisted.
